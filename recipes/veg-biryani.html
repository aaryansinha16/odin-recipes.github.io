Veg Biryani

What is Veg Biryani?

Vegetable biryani is an aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, mix veggies, herbs & biryani spices. A traditional biryani has layers of marinated meat and fragrant rice, dum cooked with caramelized onions, spices, herbs and saffron-infused milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 large onion
  • 2 fresh curry leaves
  • 1/4 medium tomato chopped
  • 1/4 cup uncooked basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/4 medium carrot, peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons frozen cut green beans, thawed
  • 1/4 medium potato, peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/4 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon biryani masala powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat until it shimmers. Cook onion and curry leaves, stirring occasionally, until onion is dark brown, 12-15 minutes. Add tomato and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, 3-5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, soak rice in water to cover for 10 minutes. Drain well.
  3. Add beans, caarrots, peas, potato, chile powder, biryani masala powder, and salt to onion mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes. Stir in drained rice and water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and cook, covered, until water is absorbed, about 30 minutes. Stir in cilantro and let stand, covered, 10 minutes before serving.
