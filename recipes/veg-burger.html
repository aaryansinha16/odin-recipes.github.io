Veg Burger

What is a veg burger?

vegetables cut into very small pieces and made into a flat round-shaped cutlet, that you eat between two pieces of bread

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups quinoa, cooked and cooled
  • ¾ cup trail mix
  • 1 large golden beet, grated
  • 1 red beet, grated
  • ½ cup whole wheat flour
  • ½ cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ½ red onion, minced
  • 5 tablespoons parsley, minced
  • 3 tablespoons tamari
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 ½ cooked, mashed sweet potatoes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
  2. Combine sweet potatoes, quinoa, trail mix, golden beet, red beet, flour, bread crumbs, oil, onion, parsley, tamari, sea salt, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a large bowl. Mix by hand until burger mix is thoroughly combined.
  3. Divide burger mix into 10 portions; press each one firmly into a patty. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
